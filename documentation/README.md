# Phase II: Business Process Modeling – SecureRehab

**Project Title:** SecureRehab – Offender Rehabilitation & Early Release Management System  
**Student:** Umwizerwa Ruth  
**Institution:** Adventist University of Central Africa (AUCA)

---

## **1. Process Scope**

SecureRehab manages offender rehabilitation, risk assessment, and early release decisions. The business process starts when an offender enters the prison system and concludes with the parole board’s decision.

The system integrates:

- Rehabilitation monitoring
- Risk evaluation
- Audit logging
- Business Intelligence (BI) reporting

This ensures informed and data-driven decision-making throughout the offender management lifecycle.

---

## **2. Actors & Responsibilities**

| Actor                      | Responsibility                                                             |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Offender**               | Participates in rehabilitation programs.                                   |
| **Intake Officer**         | Registers offenders, logs offenses, assigns initial risk scores.           |
| **Rehabilitation Officer** | Assigns and monitors rehabilitation programs, records program completion.  |
| **Security Monitor**       | Updates risk scores, reviews alerts, flags high-risk offenders.            |
| **Parole Board**           | Reviews eligibility reports and approves or denies early release.          |
| **System**                 | Calculates risk scores, generates alerts, logs audits, updates dashboards. |

---

## **3. Process Flow**

1. Offender intake and offense registration.
2. Assignment of initial risk score.
3. Allocation of rehabilitation programs.
4. Continuous monitoring of offender progress and risk levels.
5. Automatic generation of alerts if risk thresholds are exceeded.
6. Program completion review by the parole board.
7. Parole board decision on early release, concluding the process.

---

## **4. MIS & Organizational Impact**

SecureRehab enhances organizational efficiency by:

- Automating offender tracking and risk assessment.
- Supporting informed parole decisions.
- Ensuring transparency and accountability.
- Maintaining audit trails for compliance.
- Improving coordination between departments.

---

## **5. Analytics & BI Opportunities**

The system provides BI dashboards and analytics for:

- Risk trends and offender profiling.
- Rehabilitation program success rates.
- Alerts and notifications management.
- Early release eligibility tracking.

These insights support:

- Performance monitoring
- Policy improvements
- Data-driven management decisions
